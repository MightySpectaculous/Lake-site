High Country Lakes of Colorado

I have been fishing Colorado lakes since I was 2 years old. As I grew older I started hiking
higher and higher and found out that the many natural high mountain lakes can hold a excellent
fishing experience. Colorado is full of of these lakes below and above tree line, and they are the
home of many different speices of Trout that make it worth the trip.

This website is a small dedication to these wonderful lakes and fish that live within them.

Lakes below tree line

  • Rowdy Lake
  • Crystal Lake
  • Lost Lake

Lakes above tree line

  • Willis Lake
  • Hancock Lakes
  • Petroleum Lake
  • Anderson Lake

This is from a mix of Yellowstone, Western Slope, Colorado River, and a few other Cutthroat breeds.
